Agent & Landlord FAQ

What kind of people live there?

Strictly working professionals. Our average tenant is 28-35 years old, earning $85k+, who values a clean, quiet environment over a standard party house.

Is this a rooming house?

No. We host a maximum of 3 tenants per property. We operate under standard Residential Tenancies Act (RTA) leases, not rooming house regulations.

What happens if there's damage?

Zestay is the sole tenant. We are contractually liable for the property's condition and maintain a 24/7 maintenance response team for any issues.

Can you make alterations?

No structural changes are made. We may add high-quality furniture, but the property's bones remain exactly as they were when we signed.

Is this legal?

Absolutely. We use standard Victorian RTA leases with specific head-lease provisions that permit our sub-tenancy model for vetted professionals.

Do I still work with my PM?

Yes. We act as the 'Perfect Tenant'. Your PM still manages the property, collects rent from us, and conducts routine inspections. We just make their job easier.
